Where To Invest

There are many different apps that allow you to invest on the go or online through a browser. Each has its own benefits when you are just starting to invest at a young age and it is important to compare each option to figure out the best fit for you. These are some of the best starter options in 2020:

  • TD Ameritrade - Best variability between beginners and professionals
  • Robinhood - Best for free trading with no extra costs
  • Webull - Best for free commissions like Robinhood however it is less focused towards beginners
  • Wealthbase - Best for social experience as it is user friendly with competitive games
  • Invstr - Best for educating yourself on investing
